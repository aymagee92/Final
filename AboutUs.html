  • About

    Originally from Charlotte, I began practicing tax after graduating from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte in 2015 with the Bachelor's in Accounting. Over my career, I've worked with individuals and businesses that span the entire spectrum. In early 2020, my work branched out into the investing and trading world. I love focusing on the three T's - Trading, Taxes, and Technology - and enjoy guiding others in those same areas.

  • What's Your Proudest Moment?

    There's not just one. Anytime I am able to save someone money that they are entitled to, it is a great moment. The largest amount so far was requesting $30K back from the IRS after another preparer ignored their suspended losses in prior years. Even something as small as $200 from a missed Bailey exemption still stick with me because of how appreciative the clients are.

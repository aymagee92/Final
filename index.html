FAQ'S

You can find more Questions & Answers here.
  • How Much Do You Charge?

    We charge an hourly rate based on the number of hours it takes to complete the work. Before we begin work with you, we'll give you an estimated price range, and we will check in with you if we find the work to be more involved than we initially discussed.

  • Can You Save Me Money?

    Almost always, the answer is yes. Getting your tax and bookeeping work done right the first time saves you costs and time down the road. Having your investments adjusted to correctly match your risk tolerance can help reduce losses in a market downturn. Additionally, getting the work done by licensed professionals brings more knowledge and opportunities to save than with non-licensed individuals.

  • How Do I Sent You My Documents?

    Find the button at the top of the website that says "Upload Documents". This will upload your documents to our secure system.

  • How Do I Get Started?

    Send us a message using the Contact Us form below. Describe the situation you are in and the services you are in need of. We'll email you back letting you know how we can help and we'll schedule an initial consultation.
